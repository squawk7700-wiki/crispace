Next‑Gen Genetic Concepts

Imagining life that could thrive in space

CriSpace speculates on how advanced gene editing ideas—if possible—might help organisms adapt to cosmic radiation, microgravity, and other space hazards. While purely conceptual, these ideas help inspire discussion about biology beyond Earth.
